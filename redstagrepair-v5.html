FAQ

QUESTIONS WE GET EVERY DAY.

How much does a handyman charge in Los Angeles?+

Most handyman jobs in LA cost between $150 and $500. Small fixes like a door knob or small drywall patch start around $150. Bigger punch lists run $300 to $800. Red Stag gives you a flat-rate quote before any work begins.

Do I need a licensed handyman in California?+

California law requires a contractor's license for any job over $500 in combined labor and materials. Red Stag holds an active license and carries full liability insurance, so you're covered on every job regardless of size.

Can I get same-day handyman service in LA?+

Yes. Same-day service is available across most of our 29-city coverage area in LA County. Call before noon and we can usually have a tech at your door by the afternoon. Emergency service runs 24/7.

What cities does Red Stag Handyman serve?+

Red Stag Handyman serves 29 cities across Los Angeles County including Hollywood, Glendale, Burbank, Pasadena, Beverly Hills, Sherman Oaks, Santa Monica, Studio City, Long Beach, Encino, Calabasas, and more.

Is there a minimum service charge?+

Our minimum is $150, which covers the first hour of work. Most small jobs like a knob swap, small patch, or fixture install finish within that first hour. Larger jobs are quoted flat-rate before we begin.
